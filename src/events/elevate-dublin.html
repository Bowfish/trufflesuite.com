What is Truffle Elevate?

Truffle University is thrilled to host our first ever Truffle Elevate in Dublin, Ireland, from April 24 - 26 2019, at the Science Gallery Dublin!

Our three-day deep dive blockchain develpment workshop, training, and community event is designed to upskill both traditional coder and blockchain developers by empowering them to leverage Truffle's tasty blockchain development suite and connect more seamlessly to the entire blockchain ecosystem. We're hosting these 3 day workshops around the globe to meet you, the Truffle community.

Space is limited.

Elevate your development skills with our world class Truffle Presenters, Developers and Trainers!

BEN BURNS

Ben Burns currently serves as the Lead of Blockchain Services at Truffle. Since joining Truffle he’s taken over testrpc (now ganache-cli) development to improve the stability. He’s also overseen Truffle’s transition from being the Ethereum development tool of choice to the blockchain development tool of choice. He loves building simple, effective, and inexpensive solutions to very complex problems.

JOSH QUINTAL

Josh Quintal currently serves as the Lead of Front-End & Design at Truffle. Josh is passionate about where the user meets the tech; this led to the creation of two products, Truffle Boxes and Drizzle. He also created “Pet Shop”, one of Ethereum’s most used beginner tutorials. Fun fact, Josh is the most stylish member of the Truffle team.

MIKE SEESE

Although you’ll often find him wearing several hats, Mike Seese is a core developer on the Enterprise Products team. In addition to integrating the Truffle Suite to other ledgers and frameworks, he strives to bring the modern development experience to the blockchain community. A prototyper by trade, he loves piecing together cool, new projects.

NICHOLAS G. D'ANDREA

G. Nicholas D'Andrea currently serves as engineering lead for Truffle and the Truffle Debugger. Ever since 2016, he has been obsessed with the decentralized future and working towards building core software components for the blockchain ecosystem. Besides his work on Truffle, he is a core contributor to the EthPM package management system and author of EIP-1123.

KEVIN BLUER

Hailing from the UK, Kevin is Truffle’s Lead Enterprise Trainer. He’s building our world class training program from the ground up to train existing software developers to become Truffle-certified blockchain engineers. Outside of his work with Truffle, he has trained and mentored developers in cities around the globe including New York, Austin, London, Hong Kong, Shenzhen, and Kuala Lumpur.
